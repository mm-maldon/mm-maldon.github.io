ABOUT

My name is Malachi Maldonado, and I'm a third year computer science student at Santa Monica College.

My dream is to become a game developer, and use my lived experience as a Latino to tell truthful, impactful stories about marginalized communities in the games I create. In my opinion, the interactive nature of video games can allow players to experience things they never could in the real world, and that immersive power can be harnessed to cultivate empathy and understanding between all sorts of different communities and cultures.

I have experience programming in C, C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML, Python, and assembly language, and I have spent two years working as a supplemental instructor for a precalculus class. I'm planning on transferring to the University of California: Santa Cruz in Fall 2021.
